thoughts on progressive enhancement

Progressive enhancement allows all users to have an enriching experience where everyone is allowed the chance to interact with content on websites. Progressive enhancement is a modern and more acceptable way to design. Think about it... Why design something that will give users drastically different experiences depending on what device it's viewed on(like graceful degradation)? So progressive enhancement is important for all around accessability.
